Instructions

You are a lonely robot in a foggy space maze! You must find the 3 treasures hidden around the maze
in order to escape! However, due to malfunctions in your software, you can only face forward! Luckily,
you have your trusty satellite buddy to help you search. Press the UP ARROW to switch views! Click on the
3 hidden treasures, which will send you an alert when found, to win the game! Good luck!

WASD - move
IJKL - move 3rd person camera
UP ARROW - swap cameras
